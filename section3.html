PART 3 - OPERATION OF THE CAKE ACT 1993

  1. An employee's obligation to provide cakes or approved substitutes falls due on the
    following occasions:
    1. Upon the day of that employee's birthday, subject to the following caveats:
      1. if the above mentioned employee was born on the 29th of February,
        cakes fall due on the closest available working day should it not be a
        leap year;
      2. if the above mentioned employee's date of birth falls on a non-working
        day, cakes fall due on the closest available working day; and,
      3. if the above mentioned employee's date of birth falls during holidays,
        cakes fall due on the closest available working day.
    1. for employees receiving appointments or promotions, either within or outside
      the Appstablishment, cakes fall due on the closest available working day after
      confirmation of that appointment or promotion.
    2. cakes fall due upon resignation from the Appstablishment;
    3. cake fall due upon purchase of a new car, bicycle or as such;
    4. Person not getting killed qualifies as cake;
    5. cake fall due upon purchase / sell house;
    6. cake fall due upon marriage / divorce;
    7. cake fall due upon partner getting Visa approved.
