PART 2 - DEFINITIONS

Short Title

  1. For the purposes of this act an employee is defined as:
    1. A person employed in a full-time, part-time or casual capacity by Aspen Corporate.
    2. A person engaged in a contract for provision of labour to the above mentioned company.
  3. This act covers current and future employees of Appstablishment.
  5. Definitions of 'cake' and 'approved substitute' under the Act.
  7. For the purposes of this Act, 'cake' is henceforth defined as: a sweet, usually unleavened bread with other ingredients besides flour, e.g. currants, spices, eggs, sugar; or a quantity of this baked in thick disk or ornamental shape.
  8. For the purposes of the operation of this Act, the 'approved substitutes' for 'cake' are:
    1. deluxe biscuits;
    2. slices, (savoury or sweet);
    3. a generous proportion of party-pies, sausage rolls, quiches and cocktail frankfurts, hors d'oeuvres ; and,
    4. other food combinations as approved in advance by the Minister for Cake, or approved delegate.
